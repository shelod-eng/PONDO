# PONDO 2-Minute Video Script (Sponsor + Customer Demo)

## Goal
Explain how PONDO increases trust for online purchases from Amazon, Temu, Takealot, WooCommerce, and Shopify by enforcing verified checkout.

## Inputs You Already Have
- `PONDO-Payment Checkout System Spec.pdf`
- `PONDO-Payment Checkout System Spec.extracted.txt`
- Screenshots in `images/` (use these for visual cuts)

## Video Length
- 2:00 total

## Scene-by-Scene Storyboard

1. 0:00-0:12 | Opening Problem
- Visual: Hero checkout screen (Step 1, trust metrics visible)
- Voiceover:
  Online shopping is convenient, but many customers still don’t trust payment and delivery. PONDO solves this trust gap.

2. 0:12-0:28 | What PONDO Is
- Visual: PONDO logo + trust guarantee panel + partner chips
- Voiceover:
  PONDO is a trust-commerce checkout layer that integrates with Amazon, Temu, Takealot, WooCommerce, and Shopify.

3. 0:28-0:50 | Step 1 + Step 2
- Visual: Press Buy, then Confirm Details with OTP and T&C checkbox
- Voiceover:
  Step one fetches product and customer details securely from partner APIs. Step two confirms identity with OTP and POPIA consent before any sensitive processing starts.

4. 0:50-1:15 | Step 3 (Risk Controls)
- Visual: Credit & KYC checks panel
- Voiceover:
  Step three runs TransUnion ITC checks, KYC verification, Experian affordability, and fraud scoring. This ensures customers are verified and financially vetted before checkout.

5. 1:15-1:35 | Step 4 (Route + PED)
- Visual: Confirm route section with merchant/customer split and driver details
- Voiceover:
  Step four confirms payment route, final terms, and assigns PED delivery with a vetted custody chain.

6. 1:35-1:52 | Step 5 (Cleared Transaction)
- Visual: Completed state + confirmation page + sponsor dashboard
- Voiceover:
  Step five clears the transaction, logs audit evidence, updates partners, and sends customer notifications.

7. 1:52-2:00 | Closing
- Visual: PONDO trust guarantee and CTA
- Voiceover:
  PONDO turns risky online buying into verified trust commerce from checkout to delivery.
